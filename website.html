Internship Log

The theoretical concepts we covered, told as a timeline. Add an entry per week, milestone, or topic learned.

WEEK 1 · JUNE 2026

IT Infrastructure & Cybersecurity Foundation

Covered enterprise IT infrastructure (data centers, tiers, servers, storage, networking, virtualization, cloud service models IaaS/PaaS/SaaS, deployment models), networking basics (IP addressing, NAT, gateways, DNS, DHCP, VLANs, routing), core cyberthreats (phishing, malware, ransomware, credential theft, data leakage, brute-force attacks), and security layers (firewalls, email security/BEC, endpoint protection, backup & DR, MFA, network segmentation).

WEEK 2 · JUNE 2026

Infoblox, DNS, DHCP & IPAM

Studied Infoblox as the unified DDI platform (DNS+DHCP+IPAM) that secures the network's control plane. Covered DNS fundamentals (forward/reverse lookups, zones, record types, public/private/proxy DNS, DNSSEC, DoH, tunneling attacks, DDNS), DHCP mechanics (DORA process, scopes, leases, reservations, relay/IP helper, security risks), and IPAM's role in subnet planning, IP conflict prevention, and visibility. Ended with Infoblox's real use cases: centralized DNS/DHCP management, securing DNS traffic, and SIEM/firewall integration.
